Welcome to Luti Capital

Luti Capital is a forward-thinking investment project focused on the potential of digital consumer goods, gaming cosmetics, and crypto assets. What began as personal exploration has grown into an automated arbitrage engine that leverages inefficiencies across global marketplaces.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create transparent, automated strategies that help navigate emerging markets like CS:GO items, digital collectibles, and cryptocurrencies. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional finance and the growing economy of digital goods — offering access, insight, and automation for the modern investor.

Our Story

Luti Capital started in 2019, when I first encountered the world of CS:GO skins. While it seemed niche at first, I quickly recognized the underlying mechanics: liquidity, pricing, and market psychology — all operating in real-time. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I took a deep dive into the ecosystem, combining my passion for markets with my background in business informatics.

Over time, this interest evolved into building a custom arbitrage bot that now executes real trades, manages pricing, and adapts dynamically to changing conditions. The goal has always remained the same: use data, logic, and automation to identify and exploit underpriced opportunities in digital economies.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Hands-on execution: Our arbitrage bot is live and running, not just theoretical.
  • Transparency: Real profit metrics, real trades, real growth.
  • Deep domain knowledge: A unique mix of gaming culture and financial logic.
  • Independent & driven: Built from scratch, not VC-backed — every feature was earned.

Our Values

  • Rationality: We rely on logic, not hype.
  • Transparency: What we claim, we prove.
  • Curiosity: We explore new digital frontiers proactively.
  • Execution: Ideas are cheap — code and results are what matter.
