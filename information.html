Overview

Luti Capital is an independent project focused on uncovering opportunities in underpriced digital assets — with a strong focus on CS:GO skins, cases, and in-game cosmetics. Using algorithmic tools and market insights, we deploy strategies in both static long-term funds and a dynamic arbitrage engine that runs daily trades in real time.

Investment Strategy

Our strategy is twofold: we combine passive long-term funds that hold promising digital assets with an active arbitrage bot that exploits price inefficiencies across online marketplaces such as SkinBaron and BUFF.

Fund-Based Investing

  • Case Fund – Growth: Invests in discontinued CS:GO cases with limited supply. These often gain value over time due to scarcity.
  • Riptide Fund – Balanced: Diversified holdings in mid-tier skins and trade-up materials with long-term upside and more stability.

Arbitrage Bot

Our custom Python bot scans the market for temporary price mismatches and executes instant buy/sell actions. It pulls real-time data from public APIs (like /BestDeals and /NewestItems), compares against internal price oracles, and triggers profitable trades — automatically managing cooldowns, listing prices, and inventory.

Why Digital Assets?

  • Strong Upside: Skins and cosmetics have appreciated significantly in the last 5–10 years, often outperforming traditional assets.
  • Liquidity: CS:GO items can be sold instantly on multiple platforms with large trading volume.
  • Low Correlation: Prices are driven by gaming trends and user behavior, making them relatively independent from stock or crypto markets.
  • Data-Rich: Market data is transparent and accessible, enabling algorithmic trading and real-time tracking.

Our Edge

  • Speed: Automated trades executed within milliseconds of item listing.
  • Coverage: Bot runs 24/7 on a private VPS, tracking hundreds of items simultaneously.
  • Focus: We specialize in CS:GO and related digital asset markets — not general finance.
  • Rationality: We don’t follow hype. Every trade and fund decision is backed by logic and data.

What’s Next?

We continue to optimize execution speed, database architecture, and fund management. Crypto integrations and more robust pricing oracles are being explored for future expansion. The goal: scale this into a lean, automated asset operation in a space most investors still overlook.
